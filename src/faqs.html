Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use Jot?

Jot can be used by any user in need of personal reflection. Simply create an account and enjoy your personal journaling space.

Can I use Jot if I don't have a therapist?

Yes! You can register for Jot regardless of whether you have a therapist or not. However, connecting with a therapist on Jot enhances your experience and provides you with an additional support network.

Is Jot free?

Yes! Jot is free to use for patient users and therapists alike. You only need to create an account.

What features does Jot contain?

Jot contains a Journal Hub that allows users to create new entries - with daily prompts - and stores previous entries. It also contains a progress chart that illustrates self-inputted progress throughout previous months. To add another layer of support, Jot allows for therapist/patient communication at any time through a live chat. Lastly, Jot can provide personalized content recommendations based on journaled topics.

Can my therapist read all my entries?

No, you can choose to paperclip specific entries to make them private to you only. Otherwise, you can share them with your mental health professional.

Can I view old journal entries?

Yes! You can access previous journal entries through our Journal Hub's search feature.
